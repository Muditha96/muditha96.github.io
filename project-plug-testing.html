Project Overview

Testing fixture concept for plug-top checking with consistent loading, simple feedback and operator-friendly use.

Problem / Requirement

Plug checking required a repeatable method with clear feedback and less ambiguity during routine production checks.

My Role

Contributed to the test concept, fixture structure and safe documentation approach.

Engineering Approach

Considered part loading, sensing logic, operator visibility and pass/fail communication before defining the fixture logic.

Tools & Technologies Used

Testing fixtureSensorsOperator feedbackDocumentation

Result / Outcome

Improved clarity for operators and reduced uncertainty in the checking process.

Evidence

Public-safe photos, diagrams and project evidence are being prepared. Confidential factory details are not published.

What I Learned

A good testing fixture must be easy to load, hard to misuse and simple to interpret.

Public Safety Note

Only simplified descriptions and public-safe media should be shown. Do not publish PLC programs, HMI source files, wiring diagrams, exact drawings, dimensions, customer names, factory layouts, production figures or private company documents.
