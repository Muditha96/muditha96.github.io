Professional Profile

I am a Mechanical and Automation Engineer focused on solving practical factory problems by combining mechanical design, PLC systems, sensors, pneumatics, CAD design and additive manufacturing.

My portfolio is built to show evidence of practical work rather than only repeating a CV.

I'm a hands-on engineer who believes the best way to understand something is to build it, break it, and build it better. I'm drawn to innovation not as a buzzword, but as a daily habit: trying new tools, exploring new methods and going deep until I actually understand how things work.

My goal is to become a genuine expert in mechanical and automation engineering: someone who can walk into a factory problem and find a way through it.
